What is this project?

Sortify AI is an innovative solution that uses artificial intelligence to help people sort waste correctly and contribute to a more sustainable future.

Our Mission

At Sortify AI, we're on a mission to simplify waste sorting through technology. Improper waste disposal is a significant environmental challenge, with millions of tons of recyclable materials ending up in landfills each year.


Our AI-powered solution makes it easy for anyone to identify the correct bin for any waste item, helping to increase recycling rates and reduce contamination in recycling streams.


By making waste sorting accessible and accurate, we aim to contribute to a circular economy where resources are reused and recycled efficiently.

How It Helps the Environment

Our machine learning models have been trained on thousands of waste images to accurately identify different materials and their recyclability. This technology helps solve several environmental challenges:


  • Reducing Contamination: When non-recyclable items end up in recycling bins, they can contaminate entire batches, leading to more waste. Our AI helps prevent this by providing accurate sorting guidance.

  • Increasing Recycling Rates: Many people want to recycle but are unsure about which items are recyclable. By removing this uncertainty, we help increase overall recycling rates.

  • Data Collection: The data we collect helps identify trends and challenges in waste management, contributing to better recycling systems.
